Welcome to AngrySqueak Game!

Your character, Angry Squeak, is trying to avoid Gary and achieve higher levels in the game. By jumping and avoiding Gary, you can progress through the levels. You can also change the difficulty of the game and mute or unmute the background music.

Controls:

  • Press the Space key or tap the screen to make Angry Squeak jump.
  • Use the buttons at the top-right to mute/unmute music and change the difficulty level.
